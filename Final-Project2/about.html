About Us

Mission Statement

LeadiGo commits to providing a portable way of filtering lead and other harmul contents from water.

How our company was founded

In recent news about water quality in areas with low income communities, we decided to think about a solution in order to give these communities acess to clean water. We deciced to take initiative and create a product that targets these communities and ideate a solution until we finally stumbled upon one; A water bottle that primarily filters lead along with other contaminants.
